Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Most website projects take 4-8 weeks from initial consultation to launch. Brand identity projects typically take 3-6 weeks. Timeline depends on project scope and client feedback cycles.

What's included in your website design package?

Our custom website design includes responsive design for all devices, SEO optimization, user experience optimization, performance tuning, and up to 3 rounds of revisions.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes! We offer maintenance packages for updates, security, and content changes. We also provide training sessions to help you manage your site independently.

What's your pricing structure?

Pricing varies based on project scope. We offer custom quotes after understanding your needs. Contact us for a free consultation and personalized estimate.
